# Results Summary

## Dataset

- USDA Grain Basis Observations: 16,311
- Time Period: 2018–2026
- Commodities:
  - Corn
  - Soybeans
  - Hard Red Winter Wheat
  - Soft Red Winter Wheat
  - Hard Red Spring Wheat

## Corn Basis vs Dollar Index

Correlation:

-0.133

Interpretation:

A stronger U.S. Dollar Index was associated with slightly weaker corn basis levels. The relationship was negative but weak, indicating currency movements alone do not explain basis behavior.

## Conclusion

Grain basis is influenced by multiple factors including local supply-demand conditions, transportation costs, export demand, and currency strength. Dollar movements appear to contribute modestly but are not the dominant driver of basis.
